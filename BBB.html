About BBB


We are a very loud, but humble basketball family that has only one goal. To have all three Ball boys to play for the Los Angeles Lakers. Our company sells Big Baller Brand merchandise to the public; from shoes to sweaters, to awesome shirts for both male and female. We plan to branch out internationally and be one of the top companies in the world.


We have four unique people to help spread the word and make us successful. Lonzo Ball, the leader of the Ball brothers, is a successful point guard for the Lakers and is planning to be the face of the franchise. Then we have LiAngelo Ball, or what we like to call the solid brother. He has a wide frame, but uses it in the post and has a sweet three point jumper. Next is Lamelo Ball. He is a very humble player, but shows flashiness both on and off the court. Last but not least, LaVar Ball. This loud father can be annoying at times, but can be serious when it comes to his sons and the game of basketball.

Our Goal


MISSION: The key to success is to have all three of the Ball boys to play for the Lakers. It has always been a childhood dream for them and we are already a third of the way.


VISION: Our vision is called the Big Baller Vision. Our company is going to blow up and everybody in the world is goig to wear BBB accessories. We started in the U.S., then went to China, and now we're in Lithuania. Eventually, almost every country will be repping BBB. Time after time, we will have meet ups where you guys will meet the Ball family. There, you can buy our stuff, take pictures with the whole family, and possibly have a chance to go to a basketball game with the family.

PRODUCTS


We sell a variety of clothing and footwear that will be best for every customer. Each of the three ball brothers have their own signature show on sale along side with company socks, shorts, and hats. We also sell t shirts, long sleeve shirts, sweaters, and we have a Zo2 collection where we sell Lonzo's own creation of clothing. We eventually want to sell BBB jackets for the people in the cold climates, and tank tops for the people in warm climates. Our projection in this company being a top 10 clothing company is within two years. That will give us enough time to create new things, branch out, and make everybody a BIG BALLER.
